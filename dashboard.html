Inventory Performance & Stock Health Dashboard

Power BI + MySQL | Data Analytics Portfolio Project

📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes inventory performance and stock health using MySQL for data querying and Power BI for visualization. The dashboard helps monitor revenue trends, stock exposure, and inventory turnover to support data-driven decision-making.

🛠 Tools & Technologies

Power BI MySQL DAX Data Modeling Business Intelligence

📊 Key KPIs

  • Total Sales
  • Total Profit
  • Total Quantity Sold
  • Inventory Turnover
  • Growth Percentage
  • Low Stock Percentage

📈 Dashboard Features

  • Top 5 Products by Sales Performance
  • Low Stock Exposure by Category
  • Inventory Health Overview Visualization
  • Monthly Revenue Trend Analysis
  • Business Insight Panel for strategic decisions

🗄 MySQL Data Processing

  • Performed joins across orders, products, and customers
  • Aggregated total sales and profit metrics
  • Calculated monthly revenue trends
  • Generated category-wise performance insights

🎯 Business Insights

  • Inventory turnover of 9.48 indicates healthy stock movement.
  • 19% low stock exposure highlights replenishment risk in certain categories.
  • Office Supplies shows the highest low stock exposure.
